Cilantro and Ranch Dip

Description

For a light, bright, creamy sauce that is perfect for veggies, sandwiches or seafood, then you want to make this

Ingredients

  • 4 jalapenos, halved
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups loosely packed cilantro, stems removed

Steps

  1. Combine jalapenos, cilantro, sour cream, garlic, lime juice and salt in the bowl of a food processor.
  2. With the motor running, add olive oil and vinegar in a slow stream until emulsified.
