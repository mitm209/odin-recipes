Cilantro and Ranch Dip

Description

What to do with you weekend catch, fry that sucker up

Ingredients

  • 6 catfish fillets
  • 1 package of Lousiana Seasoning(Blue Pack)
  • 4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. season catfish filets and let sit for 30 minutes
  2. Heat oil to 350 degrees
  3. Cook filets 2 at a time until filet floats for 1 minute
